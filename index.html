Who are M&G?

More than 50 Branch Companies All Over The World

M&G Stationery are China’s premier stationery supplier who manufacture for top known brands in addition to their own ‘M&G’ branded range. The company has secured more than 50% of the market in China with direct sales to business and through its chain of retail outlets which covers all of China.

They were established in 1996, the company now occupies the 150,000 sq m ‘M&G Industrial Park’ in Shanghai.

With impressive double-digit growth each year for 9 years and a turnover exceeding USD$9.3 Billion in 2018 based on production figures of more than 10bn pens, pencils and associated items, M&G Stationery have risen to a level of market prominence in Asia and now seek to become established in the Australia
